Get stuffed with this stuffing!

Description

Our stuffing is going to stuff you up so good you will stay stuffed for days.

Ingredients

  • Bread of your choice
  • A nice carrot.
  • 2 half onions
  • Stuffing

Steps

  1. Chop bread into bread sized bits
  2. Stuff it into the first one
  3. Remove from oven and let sit for ten minutes.
  4. Enjoy!
