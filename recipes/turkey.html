Juicy Thanksgiving Turkey

Description

Follow this recipe in order to cook a mouth-watering, juicy turkey that will leave your Thanksgiving guests begging for more!

Ingredients

  • Parsley
  • Turkey
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Paprika
  1. Mix spices and evenly coat turkey
  2. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
  3. When oven is ready, cook turkey for 3 hours
